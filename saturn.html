WHAT IS SATURN?

Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and the second-largest in the Solar System, after Jupiter. It is a gas giant with an average radius of about nine and a half times that of Earth.It has only one-eighth the average density of Earth, but is over 95 times more massive. HOW MANY MOON SATURN HAVE?

Saturn has 83 moons. Sixty-three moons are confirmed and named, and another 20 moons are awaiting confirmation of discovery and official naming by the International Astronomical Union (IAU). Saturn's moons range in size from larger than the planet Mercury – the giant moon Titan – to as small as a sports arena.
